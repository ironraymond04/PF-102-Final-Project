PADAYON O ULI

Play


Padayon O Uli is a game where a contestant picks 1 out of 26 sealed boxes, each containing a hidden cash amount from ₱1 to ₱1,000,000. The goal is to win the highest possible prize.

In each round, the contestant opens a set number of boxes to eliminate amounts from the game. After each round, the Bangkero offers to buy their box for a cash amount based on the remaining values.

The contestant must choose to:
Padayon – Continue playing, or
Uli – Take the offer and walk away with guaranteed money.

If they reach the end without accepting an offer, they’ll decide whether to keep their original box or swap with the last one remaining. The value inside the final box becomes their prize.
